About Depistini

Our Mission

Depistini was created to empower women with knowledge and tools for breast cancer awareness and early detection. We believe that education and timely screening can save lives.

What We Offer

Our mobile application provides a comprehensive platform featuring educational content, interactive tools, and direct access to healthcare professionals.

Our Partners

We collaborate with medical institutions and associations, including the Tunisian Association for Assistance to Breast Cancer Patients, to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our content.
